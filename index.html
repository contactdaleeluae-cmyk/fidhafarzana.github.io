Good questions

The things people ask first

What exactly do you do?

I write copy and build personal brands. In practice that means your LinkedIn presence, your posts, your profile, your outreach messages, and the social content that ties it all together. One voice, everywhere your name appears.

Do you only work on LinkedIn?

No. LinkedIn is where a lot of my clients see the fastest return, but the writing and brand work travels anywhere your audience lives. We start where it matters most for you.

How do we start working together?

We talk first. You tell me where you are and where you want to be. I tell you honestly whether I can help and how. If it fits, we agree on a clear plan with no surprises.

How long until I see results?

Some things move quickly, like sharper messaging and replies to outreach. Brand presence builds steadily over weeks. I would rather set the right expectation than sell you a shortcut.

What do you need from me?

A short conversation, a bit of honesty about your goals, and your willingness to sound like the best version of yourself. I handle the words.

What does it cost?

It depends on the scope, because a single landing page and an ongoing presence are very different jobs. Tell me what you need and I will give you a clear number.

Your brand already has a voice.

Let us make it the one people remember. Tell me what you are building and where you want it to go.
